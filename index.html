Another gambling venue in Holloway Road will bring more violence to the area

Cain's Amusements Ltd has applied for an Adult Gaming Centre licence for Jackpot Slots, 403 Holloway Road, N7 6HL, at the Nag's Head. Local residents can object in writing.

Objections must reach the council by 19 July 2026

Why residents are objecting

  • 1Four betting shops and two adult gaming centres already cluster at the Nag's Head (Holloway Road/Seven Sisters Road) junction. This application adds another.
  • 2This is an area of high provision for young children: six nurseries and children's centres and a primary school sit within a few hundred metres, the nearest almost next door.
  • 3The council's own policy maps the Nag's Head (Holloway Road/Seven Sisters Road) junction as a crime and anti-social behaviour hotspot around gambling premises, with violence making up 43% of offences recorded at them, most of them during the day and early evening when children are about.

Figures come from Islington Council's Gambling Premises Licensing Policy 2023-2025, including its Local Area Profile and crime study (2019-2021).
